Color
MASTERBATCH

Color Masterbatch is a concentrated blend of pigments or dyes in a carrier resin, designed to provide vibrant, consistent coloration across a wide range of plastic applications such as extrusion, injection molding, film blowing, and lamination. Our formulations ensure excellent dispersion, thermal stability, and minimal impact on the base polymer’s mechanical properties.

Ideal for packaging, consumer goods, textiles, and industrial components, our Color Masterbatch delivers uniform color, superior visual appeal, and long-lasting colorfastness.
